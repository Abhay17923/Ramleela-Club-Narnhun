About us

The Ramleela Club Narnhun is a vibrant cultural organization based in Narnhun, India. Rooted in the ancient tradition of Ramleela, the club brings together passionate artists, actors, and enthusiasts. Their performances vividly depict episodes from the epic Ramayana, captivating audiences with colorful costumes, rhythmic music, and powerful dialogues. The club's commitment to preserving this folk theater form is commendable, as they organize annual Ramleela events during festivals like Dussehra. Through their Facebook page and Instagram page, they share glimpses of their artistic journey and engage with a wider audience. Whether you're a seasoned theater lover or a curious newcomer, the Ramleela Club Narnhun invites you to immerse yourself in the timeless tales of Lord Rama.
